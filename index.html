So Many Doors

So Many Doors is a retro puzzle platformer inspired by the Game Boy™ look & feel.
It tries to capture the aesthetics & the hardcore gameplay of those early titles, mixing them with a fresh and entertaining new twist to the platformer genre .

Try to find your way through the doors maze.
Move from start to final door. Use portals.
Choose wisely; some doors are good, others not at all.
Remember the path, avoid failing too many times.
Be fast, and most important, don't die! If you can!
